About me

There is no true model to save your data, just some useful ones. Those ones are our main goal: find a model that can be useful for your problem. In this webapp, I can help you find this model by providing a simple interface to train a model, explore the basics of it and finally score some test data.

It constitutes an end-to-end example to approach a real-world text mining example. For now, just unsupervised models are used here, but this webapp could be easily tailored for supervised ones.

Please, feel free to play with this experiment, improve it and learn a lot from it!
